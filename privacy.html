Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

The short version: we don't sell your data, we don't track you, and we don't use cookies. Images you scan are sent to third-party AI models for analysis and are not stored by us.

Who we are

Essential is an AI image detection service available at essentialai-app.com. It helps you identify whether an image was generated by AI.

For privacy questions, contact us at: privacy@essentialai-app.com

What data we collect

If you use the site as a guest

  • Nothing is stored on our servers. Your scan count is kept in your browser's local storage only.

If you create an account

  • Your email address
  • Your name and profile picture if you sign in with Google
  • Your scan quota (how many scans you have used) stored in our database

If you make a payment

  • Your payment is processed entirely by Stripe. We never see or store your card number.
  • We receive confirmation of whether a payment succeeded and which plan you purchased.

Images you scan

When you scan an image, it is sent to two third-party AI analysis services to determine whether it was AI-generated. The image is transmitted over an encrypted connection.

First, your image is analysed by Sightengine. When Sightengine returns a high-confidence result, the image is also sent to Anthropic (Claude) as a second opinion. The two scores are averaged to produce the final result.

We do not store your images on our servers. Each service's own privacy policy governs how they handle images on their end. Based on their documentation, images are not retained after analysis.

Cookies

We do not use cookies. We use browser local storage strictly for functional purposes: keeping you signed in, remembering your scan count, and storing your scan history locally on your device. This data never leaves your browser unless you are signed in, in which case your scan quota is synced to our database.

Third-party services

  • Supabase - authentication and scan quota database. Data is stored on servers in the EU. Privacy policy
  • Sightengine - primary AI image analysis. Images are sent here for every scan. Privacy policy
  • Anthropic (Claude) - secondary AI image analysis. Images are sent here as a second opinion when Sightengine returns a high-confidence result. Privacy policy
  • Stripe - payment processing. Card data is handled entirely by Stripe. Privacy policy
  • Vercel - website hosting. Standard server logs (IP address, request metadata) are retained briefly for security purposes. Privacy policy

How long we keep your data

  • Account data is kept for as long as your account is active.
  • Scan history is stored only in your browser's local storage and is never sent to our servers.
  • If you delete your account, your data is removed from our database within 30 days.

Your rights

If you are in the EU or UK, you have rights under GDPR including:

  • Access - you can request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Deletion - you can request that we delete your account and associated data
  • Correction - you can request that we correct inaccurate data
  • Portability - you can request your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@essentialai-app.com.

Children

Essential is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
