**The ****Crucial**** ****Rule****: ****Avoid**** ****the**** "API Cost ****Trap****"**

Before choosing how to get paid, you must understand your expenses:

- Every time a user checks an image, your external AI brain (like Sightengine) charges you, for example, $0.003.

- If a single user uploads 1,000 images for fun, they just cost you $3.00.

- Therefore, **you**** ****cannot**** ****offer**** ****unlimited**** free ****use****.** You must gate the app.

**The 2 Best ****Monetization**** ****Models**** ****for**** ****Your**** ****App**

**1. The ****Token****/Credit System (****Highly**** ****Recommended****)**

Instead of a monthly subscription, users buy "credits."

- **How**** it ****works****:** Everyone gets 3 free scans when they first open the app. If they want more, they buy a package (e.g., $4.99 for 100 scans).

- **Why**** ****it’s**** ****great**** ****for**** ****you****:** It directly matches your costs. If they don't pay, they can't burn through your API budget.

**2. The ****Premium**** ****Weekly****/****Monthly**** ****Subscription**

Users pay a recurring fee for higher access.

- **How**** it ****works****:** You offer a weekly subscription (e.g., $2.99/week) or monthly (e.g., $9.99/month) for up to 500 scans per month.

- **Why**** ****it’s**** ****great**** ****for**** ****you****:** Recent data shows mobile users love weekly micro-subscriptions, providing you with a predictable, recurring income.

**What**** is an "****Operation****"?** In your app's case, 1 operation = 1 image frame uploaded and analyzed.

**The Profit ****Math****: ****How**** ****Much**** ****Do**** ****You**** ****Make****?**

Let's do the exact math to see how much money goes into your pocket using the **Starter ****Plan** ($29/mo) and selling **Credit ****Packs** to your users.

Imagine a user runs out of free scans and buys your **$4.99 ****package**** ****for**** 100 ****scans**.

- **Your**** ****Revenue****:** +$4.99

- **Payment**** ****Processing**** ****Fee** (Lemon Squeezy / Stripe takes roughly 5% + $0.30): -$0.55

- **The API Cost** (100 scans × $0.0020 per extra scan): -$0.20

- **Your**** Net Profit:** **+$4.24**

As you can see, the profit margins on "AI Wrapper" apps are incredibly high. A single customer buying a $4.99 pack completely covers your API costs for that user and leaves you with a great margin.

**How**** ****to**** ****Keep**** an ****Eye**** ****on**** ****Costs**** ****Automatically**

Because you have zero coding skills, you might worry about a user finding a loophole and running up your bill. You can have Claude build safety guardrails into your app right from the start.

**The "Cost ****Control****" Prompt ****for**** Claude:**

When Claude is writing your backend code, give it this exact instruction:

*"Add a **hard** limit **to** **the** backend **code**. No **single** **user** ID is **allowed** **to** **make** more **than** 5 API **requests** per **day** **unless** **their** **database** **profile** shows **they** **have** **purchased** **premium** credits. **If** **they** hit **the** limit, stop **the** API **call** **entirely** and **send** back a 'Limit **Reached**' **message** **to** **the** frontend."*

By doing this, your app will block the request *before* it ever reaches Sightengine, meaning you will never be charged for unexpected spam. Furthermore, your Sightengine developer dashboard will show you a live, real-time chart of exactly how many cents you have spent so far that month.
