Render is a cloud hosting platform mentioned as an alternative to [[Vercel]] for deploying Essential's backend. Both connect to [[GitHub]] and auto-deploy on push.

## Key Facts
- Alternative to Vercel for hosting the Node.js backend
- Also connects to GitHub for continuous deployment
- Free tier available
