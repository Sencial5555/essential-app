Hive Moderation is a third-party content moderation API. In the Essential project it is the alternative candidate (alongside [[Sightengine]]) for the AI image detection engine.

## Key Facts
- Role in Essential: potential backend detection API; final selection between Hive and Sightengine not yet decided
- Used via Image/Frame detection endpoints
