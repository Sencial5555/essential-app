## Overview
Sightengine is a third-party image and video moderation API. In the Essential project it is the primary candidate for the AI image detection engine, used via Image/Frame detection endpoints.

## Key Facts
- Role in Essential: backend API call target for determining whether an uploaded image is AI-generated
- Listed alongside [[Hive Moderation]] as the two candidate providers; final choice not yet decided in source
