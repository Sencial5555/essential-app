## Summary
Platform-by-platform marketing strategy for launching Essential. Core rule: be the authentic founder, not a fake customer. Each platform gets a tailored angle. Claude can write the actual posts and scripts.

## Key Points
- Golden rule: never pretend to be a random customer who "found" the app — Reddit/TikTok audiences see through it immediately.
- TikTok: "Zero-Skills Underdog" angle — screen record showing app detecting a viral AI video frame.
- Reddit: r/SideProject, r/InternetIsBeautiful — story post asking for feedback, not promotion.
- X/Twitter: "Build in Public" threads — share Claude prompts, ugly early designs, revenue milestones.
- LinkedIn: "Future of Work" case study — non-technical person becomes software creator via AI.
- Claude can write all content: TikTok scripts, Reddit posts, LinkedIn articles.
