## Summary
Rationale for naming the app "Essential," analysis of domain options, three polished motto variations, hero section layout mockup, and social media hook scripts based on the founder's own story.

## Key Points
- "Essential" chosen for its premium, minimalist, tech-forward feel — aligns perfectly with [[Calm Design]].
- .com domain likely unavailable; modern alternatives: essential.ai, essentialapp.co, getessential.io.
- Three motto options: "Trust what you see," "Sifting the real from the AI generated," "Your lens for a synthetic world."
- Hero section: brand name + motto + large centered drop-zone + quota progress bar — nothing else.
- Social media hook: founder story ("I have zero coding skills, but I got sick of fake AI videos...") outperforms fake "found this cool app" approach.
