## Summary
High-level case for why Essential is worth building now: low upfront risk, strong market timing (deepfake/misinformation wave), and a compelling founder story. Frames the three pillars of execution.

## Key Points
- Three execution pillars: Technical (MVP first, bulletproof billing), Marketing (ride deepfake news wave + Zero-Skills Underdog story), Financial (never lose money on a scan, hard rate limits).
- MVP definition: one thing flawlessly — user uploads image, gets accurate AI probability score. No extras on day one.
- Startup costs: Claude Pro $20/month + domain $10/year + API starter tier. Total risk is minimal.
- Even if the project earns only a few hundred dollars/month, it's a cash-flowing digital asset.
- The timing is optimal: deepfakes, fake news, and AI misinformation are peak-concern topics across all platforms.
