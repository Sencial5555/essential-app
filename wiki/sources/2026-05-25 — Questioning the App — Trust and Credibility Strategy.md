## Summary
How to handle user skepticism about a non-coder's AI detection app. Strategy: don't claim the AI as your own invention — position the app as a premium bridge to enterprise-grade APIs. Transparency becomes a trust asset.

## Key Points
- Cocktail bar framing: "I didn't distill the vodka — I combined premium ingredients into a convenient experience."
- Pitch: "An easy mobile wrapper that connects you to the world's most accurate enterprise AI-detection networks."
- API credibility inheritance: users trust Hive Moderation/Sightengine, which are multi-million dollar companies with teams of data scientists.
- Transparency hook: "I have zero coding skills, which is exactly why I didn't try to build my own AI model — I used Claude to plug into enterprise APIs."
- Trust Section on landing page: SSL encryption, no permanent data storage, enterprise-grade AI infrastructure, 99% uptime guarantee.
