## Definition
The technical capability of determining whether a given image was generated by an AI system (e.g. diffusion model, GAN) or captured by a real camera. In [[Essential]], this is provided as a service via third-party APIs rather than a proprietary model.

## Why It Matters
This is the entire product value of Essential. As AI-generated imagery becomes indistinguishable to the human eye, a fast, accessible detection tool has clear consumer demand.

## Evidence & Examples
- Essential uses [[Sightengine]] or [[Hive Moderation]] Image/Frame detection endpoints
- Results expressed as probability scores, mapped to [[Traffic-Light Results]] color states
- Scan latency: 1–2 seconds expected (per scanning animation timing in source)

## Tensions & Contradictions
- Choice between Sightengine and Hive Moderation not yet resolved — accuracy, pricing, and API limits not compared in ingested sources. This is an open decision.
