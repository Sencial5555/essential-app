## Definition
Minimum Viable Product — the smallest version of [[Essential]] that delivers core value: a user uploads an image and receives an accurate AI probability score. Nothing else on day one.

## Why It Matters
Starting with MVP prevents scope creep, keeps Claude sessions short and cheap, and gets something real into users' hands fast. A working app with one feature beats a broken app with ten.

## What's In the MVP
- Image upload drop-zone
- API call to [[Sightengine]] or [[Hive Moderation]]
- [[Traffic-Light Results]] display
- Free tier (3 anonymous scans → login gate)

## What's NOT in the MVP
- Multiple subscription tiers
- Advanced analytics
- Mobile app (web-first is sufficient)
- User dashboard
