## Definition
The name, motto, domain, and visual positioning of [[Essential]] as a premium consumer product for the AI-detection space.

## Name
**Essential** — chosen for being punchy, minimalist, and premium. A common dictionary word, which means the .com domain is likely unavailable or very expensive.

## Domain Options
- essential.ai (preferred — tech-forward)
- essentialapp.co
- getessential.io

## Motto Options
Three candidates (none yet finalized):
1. **"Trust what you see."** — direct and bold
2. **"Sifting the real from the AI generated."** — social media focus
3. **"Your lens for a synthetic world."** — modern and urgent (used in the foundational blueprint)
